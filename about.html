About Us

Welcome to UK Salary Calculator, a simple utility website designed to help UK employees, freelancers, and job seekers quickly convert their earnings.

Our Mission

Many salary calculators are cluttered with ads, pop-ups, and unnecessary questions. We wanted to create something different:

  • Fast: No page reloads. Results appear as you type.
  • Private: Your salary data never leaves your browser.
  • Transparent: We explain exactly how the numbers are worked out.
  • Accessible: Designed to work well on mobiles, tablets, and desktops.

Who is this for?

This tool is perfect for:

  • Converting an annual salary offer into an hourly rate.
  • Freelancers working out what an hourly rate equals annually.
  • Budget planning by seeing weekly and daily breakdowns.
