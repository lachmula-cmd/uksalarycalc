3. UK Calculator Specific Notes

Our UK salary calculator uses HMRC Income Tax bands for the 2025/26 tax year. It calculates Income Tax only. It does not include:

  • National Insurance contributions
  • Pension contributions (auto-enrolment or otherwise)
  • Student loan repayments
  • Any other payroll deductions

The calculator assumes the standard Personal Allowance (£12,570) and does not account for Personal Allowance tapering above £100,000, adjusted tax codes, or other individual circumstances.

4. EU and Global Calculator Specific Notes

Our EU and Global country calculators provide simplified gross-to-net estimates based on national/federal tax rates and standard deductions. These calculators:

  • Use publicly available 2024 tax rates (or most recently available)
  • Apply standard personal allowances and deductions only
  • Do not include state, provincial, regional, cantonal, or local taxes
  • Do not account for filing status variations beyond a single standard filer
  • Do not include employer-specific benefits, deductions, or arrangements
  • Are based on simplified models that may not reflect the full complexity of each country's tax system

These estimates are intended to give a general indication of gross-to-net pay for comparison purposes only. They should not be used for financial planning, salary negotiation, or tax compliance purposes.
