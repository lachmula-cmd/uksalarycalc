About SalaryUKCalc

An independent salary and tax information resource covering the UK, EU, and global economies. Free to use, transparent about how it works, and honest about what it can and cannot tell you.

What Is SalaryUKCalc?

SalaryUKCalc.com is a free, independent website providing salary and tax calculators, guides, and informational content for employees, freelancers, and job seekers. We started as a UK-focused salary calculator and have expanded to cover EU countries and major global economies.

We are not a financial services firm, a tax advisory service, or an accountancy practice. We are an independent information resource — our goal is to help people understand their pay, not to replace professional advice.

Our Mission

Salary and tax information is often locked behind jargon, paywalls, or buried in government documentation. We believe everyone deserves clear, accessible information about how their pay is calculated — regardless of where they live or work.

Our mission is to provide:

  • Clear, honest calculators — with transparent methodology and clear labelling of estimates
  • Useful guides — that explain concepts in plain English without assuming prior knowledge
  • International coverage — so you can compare salaries across the UK, EU, and beyond
  • Privacy by design — all calculations run in your browser; we never see your salary data

Our Principles

Honesty about limitations
We clearly label all EU and Global results as estimates. We explain what is and is not included in each calculation. We do not pretend our simplified models are precise tax calculations.
No invented facts
All tax rates, thresholds, and allowances are sourced from official government publications. We cite our sources on the Methodology page. We do not invent or approximate tax facts.
Privacy by design
All calculations run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. We never collect, store, or transmit any salary figures or personal financial data you enter. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Accessible and fast
No registration required. No paywalls. No unnecessary questions. Results appear instantly. The site works on mobile, tablet, and desktop.
Transparent methodology
We publish our full calculation methodology, data sources, and assumptions on the Methodology page. You can see exactly how every number is produced.

Who Is This Site For?

SalaryUKCalc is useful for:

  • Employees wanting to understand their gross-to-net pay breakdown
  • Job seekers comparing salary offers in different countries
  • Freelancers converting between hourly rates and annual salary equivalents
  • People relocating internationally who want a rough sense of take-home pay
  • Students and graduates understanding their first payslip
  • Anyone curious about how income tax and social contributions work

Important Limitations

SalaryUKCalc is an informational resource, not a professional tax service. Our calculators have important limitations:

  • UK calculator: Income Tax only — does not include National Insurance, pension, or student loan
  • EU and Global calculators: national/federal rates only — state, provincial, and local taxes not included
  • All calculators assume standard deductions and a single standard filer — personal circumstances vary
  • Tax rates change regularly — always verify with the relevant official authority

For advice specific to your situation, always consult a qualified tax professional or your country's official tax authority. See our full disclaimer for more detail.

How the Site Is Funded

SalaryUKCalc.com is free to use and is supported by advertising through Google AdSense. Advertisements are clearly separated from content. We do not accept sponsored content, paid placements, or affiliate arrangements that could compromise the independence of our information.
