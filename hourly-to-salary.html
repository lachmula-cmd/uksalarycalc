Frequently Asked Questions

How is the annual salary calculated?

We multiply your hourly rate by the number of hours you work a week, and then multiply that by the weeks worked in a year (usually 52).

Does this figure include holiday pay?

If you are a full-time employee, your annual salary usually includes paid holiday. For contractors or zero-hour workers, this calculates your gross earnings if you work 52 weeks.

What about National Minimum Wage?

This calculator accepts any hourly rate. Always ensure your hourly rate meets the current UK National Minimum Wage or Living Wage standards.

Is this calculation accurate for taxes?

No. This shows your gross income. Your take-home pay will be lower after tax and National Insurance deductions.

Why ask for working days per week?

We use this purely to estimate your "Daily Pay". It does not affect the calculation of your annual salary.
